School Campus
Built in 1994

Gateway Middle School opened in 1994. Eight classrooms were added in 2004, thanks to voter support of schools and students. The classrooms, commons, gymnasiums and fields are regularly scheduled for community organizations seeking well-maintained space for their events. Built in an area that is designated as a natural habitat, much of the original landscape is in place.

family technology support

  • Need Help?

    For computer repair issues, please select the link for Appointments on the left menu to schedule a time to bring the computer in. For issues not related to broken hardware, please use the Quicklink to the right for Family Tech Help Request ticket. Be as detailed about your need for support and a member of our technical team will contact you to assist. Call in during school hours to 425-385-4357 (HELP) Thank you for your ongoing partnership in these unusual times.

  • New Student Enrollment

    Welcome to Everett Public Schools. We are using Online Enrollment to assist in enrolling new students. This system allows parents to enroll their students using any computer or mobile device, which takes approximately 30 minutes. If you need to find which school your child should attend, please select the Bus Routes icon on top of the page, click Find My School and Route. If you have multiple children, the system will give you the option to share some of the information from the first enrollment saving time. The system saves packets in process, so you can exit and return to complete the process later.
